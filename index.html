Design is my passion

My name is Louisa. I’m a designer and front-end developer. I love modern, clean, pixel-perfect websites and multimedia interfaces. Whether it is designing and implementing each project by myself or working with a team, I like to take a hands on approach in order to identify with the product. I particuarly enjoy seeing concepts through to completion by using innovative technology.

What I'm doing now

I am solely responsible for the conceptualization and design of web and print solutions for several hundred automotive and motorcycle dealerships. Internally, I help guide my company’s visual brand identity. In addition, I direct and review the work of junior designers to ensure that designs are top-notch.
